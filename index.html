FranceConnect: Simplifying Access to Public Services

Introduction

In today's fast-paced digital world, accessing public services online has become an increasingly common and convenient option. However, navigating through multiple websites and login portals can be a daunting task, often requiring multiple registrations and passwords. To address this challenge, the French government introduced FranceConnect, a secure and streamlined platform that simplifies user access to a wide range of online public services.

The Need for a Streamlined Public Service Access Solution

With the increasing digitization of public services, the need for a centralized and user-friendly access solution became evident. Users often faced difficulties in remembering multiple login credentials, navigating through various websites, and understanding complex application processes. This resulted in frustration, delays, and a barrier to accessing essential services.

Introducing FranceConnect

In 2016, the French government launched FranceConnect, a centralized authentication and authorization platform that provides users with a single sign-on (SSO) solution for accessing a wide range of online public services. With FranceConnect, users can securely log in to various government websites using their existing credentials from trusted identity providers, such as La Poste, Tax.gouv.fr, or Ameli.fr.
